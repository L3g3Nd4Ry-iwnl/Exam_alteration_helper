Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? We are here to answer. If you don't see your question here, drop us a line through the form below

1. I've forgot my password. How can I login?

ANSWER: Click the forgot password link on the login page and enter your registered e-mail address.You will recieve a reset link to your mail.

2. Can I update my profile?

ANSWER: You can change them by clicking the edit details link in your dashboard.

3. What are the info about myself that I can update?

ANSWER: You can change a lot of things once you login to your dashboard.You can change your display picture,e-mail address,password and contact details.

4. How can I change my password?

ANSWER: Once you login to your dashboard click on the edit details button and then click change my password.Enter your old password and then enter your password.Your new password will be updated.

5. How to exchange substitution slots?

ANSWER: In your dashboard,click on the Exchange Slot button.It will have the list of available faculties.Select one slot of the other faculty and request confirmation for replacement.
